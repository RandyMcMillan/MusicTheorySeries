Music Theory 101

Music Theory 101 is a work in progress. Thank you for your continued interest in this app. Please email me with constructive feedback. Your input will be considered as I continue to add more features and content to the app. I especially welcome feedback from anyone who may have commented on the app in iTunes. I am particularly interested in discussing:

- Wikipedia: Hermann_von_Helmholtz#Acoustics_and_aesthetics

- Wikipedia: Polymodal Chromaticism

While I these topics seem to be complex in nature and elusive to a lot of students of music theory. I insist that they are not. The simple approach presented in this app and any future supplementary apps (Music Theory 102, etc) will yield a different perspective and application to concepts that normally take years to internalize and master. The objective of this app is to explain these (and other concepts) specifically.
